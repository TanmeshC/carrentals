Rental Service Terms and Conditions

1. Booking and Reservation

All bookings are subject to availability. Reservations can be made in advance, and it is recommended to book early to ensure availability of the desired vehicle.

2. Rental Period

The rental period begins from the designated start date and time specified during the booking process. The rental period ends on the agreed end date and time. Any extension of the rental period must be communicated and approved in advance.

3. Age Requirement

Cancellations made within a specified timeframe may be subject to a cancellation fee. The cancellation policy will be communicated during the booking process.
